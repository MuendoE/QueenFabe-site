Our Story

Where Johannesburg's next beauty pros are made.

Where It Started

Founded in a Johannesburg studio, not a boardroom.

Queen of Fabulousness started with a simple observation: talented people across Joburg were teaching themselves makeup, lashes, and nails from phone screens, with no studio time and no one to correct their hand. So we built the studio — small classes, real client practice, and instructors who are still working artists themselves, not just teachers reading from a manual.

The shop came next, almost by accident. Students kept asking where to buy the exact brushes and products we used in class — so now anyone can, whether or not they've ever set foot in the studio.
